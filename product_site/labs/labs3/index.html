Favorite Dish: Chicken Quesadilla


A chicken quesadilla is a warm, golden tortilla filled with melted cheese and seasoned chicken, grilled until crisp on the outside and gooey inside. Originating from Mexico, the word quesadilla literally means “little cheesy thing,” and it has been enjoyed for centuries as a simple yet satisfying dish.
I enjoyed Quesadillas a lot as a kid, making it a very memorable dish for me.

Quesadilla Ingredients
Ingredient Quantity
Flour Tortillas 4 Medium
Cheddar Cheese 8 tbsp., Shredded
Monterey Jack Cheese 8 tbsp, Shredded
Chicken Breast 1 small, chopped
Onion 1 small, diced
Red Bell Peper 1 small, diced
Tomato 1 small, diced
Salt & Pepper 1/4 tsp. for each
Olive Oil 1 tbsp.



Chocolate Mousse


Chocolate mousse is a light, airy dessert made by folding whipped cream or egg whites into rich, melted chocolate, creating a smooth and fluffy texture. Originating in France in the 18th century, “mousse” means “foam,” perfectly describing its delicate consistency.



Chocolate Mousse Ingredients
Ingredient Quantity
Dark Chocolate (70%) 150g
Egg 6
Golden Sugar 30g
Salt One Pinch

Steak Burrito


A steak burrito is a hearty, flavor-packed wrap that combines tender, seasoned steak with rice, beans, cheese, and fresh toppings, all snugly rolled in a warm flour tortilla. Rooted in Mexican cuisine and popularized in the U.S., burritos became beloved for their portability and satisfying mix of flavors.



Steak Burrito Ingredients
Ingredient Quantity
Large Flour Tortilla 1
Seasoned Steak 1
Rice 1 Box
Black Beans 1 Can
Shredded Cheese 1
Salsa 1 Can
Sour Cream 1 Bottle
Guacamole or Avocado 1
Chopped Lettuce 1 Lettuce Head
