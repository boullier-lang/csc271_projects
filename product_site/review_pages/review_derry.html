It: Welcome to Derry

It: Welcome to Derry is a chilling, atmospheric horror‑mystery series that peels back the hidden dread at the heart of a small town. The story begins in 1962, when a young boy disappears in the dead of night, and shortly after a family moves into town — unaware they’re stepping into something far darker than they imagined. As strange vanishings multiply and whispers of a lurking evil spread, the quiet streets of Derry become haunted by fear, suspicion, and memories too painful to forget. Neighbors turn watchful, friends drift into paranoia, and every shadow breeds uncertainty as the residents grapple with unanswerable questions and creeping dread. With each revelation, the boundary between reality and nightmare blurs — as terror emerges not just from outside, but from the secrets buried within. It: Welcome to Derry is a harrowing journey into innocence lost, ancient evil reawakened, and the fragile hope that light can survive when darkness reigns.
