Baldur's Gate 3

 Baldur’s Gate 3  is a next‑generation role‑playing game developed by Larian Studios, set in the famed Forgotten Realms of the Dungeons & Dragons universe. You begin the story abducted aboard a nautiloid ship, infected with a parasitic tadpole that threatens to turn you into a mind flayer, while simultaneously awakening strange new powers. As the corruption grows, you must forge your own path—resist the darkness and try to remove the parasite, or embrace the power it offers and reshape your destiny. Along the way you’ll gather companions, explore shadow‑cursed forests, the depths of the Underdark, and the sprawling city of Baldur’s Gate itself, all while caught in a brewing conflict between devils, deities and otherworldly threats. Your choices, alliances and actions leave a lasting mark on the world—this is a tale of survival, power, identity and legacy.
