Clair Obscur: Expedition 33

Clair Obscur: Expedition 33 is a next‑generation exploration and survival adventure, plunging players into the mysterious depths of an uncharted subterranean world. You begin the expedition trapped within a labyrinthine facility, surrounded by enigmatic machinery and corridors that shift with shadows, while uncovering a mysterious phenomenon that threatens both mind and body. As the darkness deepens, you must choose your path—investigate the source of the anomaly, or harness its power to bend the environment to your will. Along the way you’ll encounter strange companions, navigate perilous caverns and shadowed ruins, and uncover hidden archives and relics, all while piecing together the history of Expedition 33 and the secrets it conceals. Your choices, observations, and actions leave indelible marks on the unfolding story—this is a tale of discovery, danger, ingenuity, and the haunting beauty of light and shadow.
