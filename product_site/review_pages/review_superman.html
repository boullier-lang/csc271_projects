Superman (2025)

Superman (2025) is a bold, modern‑day superhero epic from James Gunn’s reimagined DC universe. The story begins when Clark Kent, a man with extraordinary gifts, tries to balance life in Metropolis with a secret identity — all while threats loom on the horizon and hope becomes a fragile commodity. As tensions mount, a powerful adversary emerges, dragging the world into a conflict between ideals, truth, and power, forcing Clark to confront not only external danger, but the responsibilities and weight of being a symbol. Along the way, truths are tested, alliances formed and betrayed, and the line between heroism and sacrifice blurs. Superman becomes more than a savior — he becomes a beacon of hope in a world torn by fear, doubt, and corruption. “Superman (2025)” is a story of legacy, identity, and the enduring fight for what’s right when darkness threatens to define everything.
